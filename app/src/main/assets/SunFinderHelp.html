How to use SunFinder!

 SunFinder allows you to bookmark your favorite cities and lookup their current as well as forecasted weather.

  • Home Page 

    • Display the first city's today's weather from you list along with your favorite cities. You can hide the first city's weather to see just the cities list by dragging the red bar.

  • Bookmarking a city 

    • Click "Add Place" button in the top of the app. That will take you to a map view.
    • Find your place by zooming in and click on your city of choice.
    • That will add the city to your bookmarked list.

  • Viewing current and forecasted weather

    • To view current and forecasted weather just click on the city name in the list of booked marked cities. 

  • Deleting a bookmarked city

    • To delete a city from the list click on the trash can provided next to the city name.
